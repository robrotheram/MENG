Welcome to the help section.

Site needs:

  • Modern Day Browser
  • Must Support WebGL, If in doubt go here: https://get.webgl.org/

How to use the site

  • View the Jet Engine learn about each part
  • Click and drag to rotate the Jet Engine
  • View the Materials Section
  • Click on choose and drag the right material to the correct part
  • View the Check
  • Click and drag the material image to the box under the Jet Engine component
  • Once you have chosen your selection click the check button to see if you are correct
